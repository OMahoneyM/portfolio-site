Welcome to Michael's portfolio.

As an 8-year-old, I shocked my family when I sat on the living room floor and took apart our family computer. It was a fun little puzzle learning what the pieces were and how they combined to form the sophisticated machine I loved playing with after school. To their relief, it still worked when I put it back together.

Previously a Smithsonian Bioinformatician, I am always thinking about solutions to coding and big data challenges. But more simply, I am a problem solver. It doesn't matter the medium. Whether it's wrenching on my Swedish basket case of a car, sitting down with a friend in need, or coding better genomic data infrastructure, I like to know that I am improving things around me in the simple ways I can.
